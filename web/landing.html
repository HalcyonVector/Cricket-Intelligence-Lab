The dashboard

Everything the scorecard leaves out

Pick any of the 48 competitions and the same depth follows it. Every batter and bowler is ranked, sortable and one click from a full profile.

🎯

Player profiles

Click a name and you get percentile ranks, powerplay / middle / death splits, opposition and venue breakdowns, and a career timeline that moves.

🏆

Records that re-sort

Most runs, most wickets, best averages and strike rates. Click a column and the ranking follows it, era-adjusted where the era matters.

🕸️

Who bats like who

Pick a batter and the dashboard surfaces their closest statistical neighbours, scored across twelve metrics.

⚖️

Four-way compare

Under the hood

Why it opens instantly.

Nothing is calculated in your browser. Every number is worked out ahead of time and saved, so the dashboard is only ever reading finished results.

1

Ingest

Every Cricsheet match is streamed into SQLite, one delivery at a time.

2

Compute

Percentiles, splits, similarity and era indices are calculated once, across all 48 cohorts.

3

Bundle

Each competition is saved as its own ~500 KB file, checked for correctness before it ships.

4

Open

Your browser just loads the file. No backend to wait on, no database to spin up.
